About YesYing

YESYING is located in Jiangmen City, west of the Pearl River Delta, one of the biggest hardware manufacturing bases in China.

Our company specializes in exporting stainless steel, metal wire and iron houseware, including kitchen, hotel and bathroom accessories. Our products are sold to over 30 countries in Europe, North and South America, Asia and Australia.

We insist on the principle of "Keep improving & Customer satisfaction". We keep innovating and adopt advanced production and management experience to develop new products continuously.
